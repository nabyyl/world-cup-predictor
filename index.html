Rules

Scoring Rules

Simple rule: make your picks before kickoff, collect match points, and earn extra bonus points during the tournament.

5 Max points per match
⚽

1. Match Prediction Points

Each match can give a maximum of 5 points.

3 pts Exact score
1 pt Correct one team’s score
1 pt Correct winner, draw, or advanced team
1 pt Correct first team to score or no goal
Example

You predict France 2 - 0 Senegal. Actual score is France 2 - 1 Senegal. You get 1 point for correctly predicting France’s score.

🎯

2. Winner / Advance Rule

This is the 1-point result pick.

  • Select the team you think will win.
  • For group matches, you may select Draw.
  • For knockout matches, select the team you think will advance.
  • If a knockout match is drawn and decided by penalties, Super Admin will select the team that advanced.
  • Users who selected the advanced team will get the winner/advance point.
Example

You predict France 1 - 1 Senegal and select France to advance. If France wins on penalties, you can still receive the winner/advance point.

🥅

3. First Team to Score / No Goal

This is worth 1 point per match.

  • Select the team you think will score first.
  • If you think the match will end 0-0, select No goal / 0-0.
  • This is separate from your score prediction.
  • For a 0-0 knockout match, Super Admin can still select the team that advanced separately.
🎁

4. End-of-Tournament Major Bonuses

Maximum major bonus points: 80.

30 pts Tournament Winner
20 pts Golden Boot / Top Scorer
15 pts Finalist Team A
15 pts Finalist Team B
  • Finalist predictions are order-free.
  • If you pick France and Brazil, you still get the finalist points even if the actual final is Brazil vs France.
🏆

5. Stage Bonus Questions

Maximum stage bonus points: 50.

Group Stage 5 points each
  • Team with most goals during group stage
  • Team that concedes the fewest goals during group stage
  • Team with the most yellow cards during group stage
Round of 32 7 points

Which match will go to extra time?

Round of 16 7 points

Which team will win through a penalty shootout?

Quarter-finals 7 points

Which team will progress with a clean sheet?

Semi-finals 7 points

Which team will have the most possession?

Final 7 points

How many total goals will be scored in the first half of the Final?

Final first-half example

If the Final is 1 - 1 at half-time, the correct answer is 2. This bonus is based only on total first-half goals, not which team scores.

🔒

6. Lock Rules

Once locked, predictions cannot be edited.

  • Match predictions lock automatically at kickoff.
  • You can edit a match prediction unlimited times before kickoff.
  • The last saved prediction before kickoff is the one that counts.
  • Bonus sections can be locked separately by Super Admin.
  • Closed bonus sections move to the bottom of the Bonus page.
  • Super Admin can reopen a match only if needed.
📊

7. Total Score

Leaderboard points are calculated automatically.

Total Points = Match Points + Bonus Points
5 Max points per match
130 Max bonus points
80 Major bonus points
50 Stage bonus points
🌐

8. Fixtures and Live Info

Fixtures are loaded from the local FIFA 2026 portal schedule file.

  • Use the View on Google link on any match to open Google’s live match card.
  • Super Admin can update scores manually.
  • Super Admin scores are protected from auto-sync when marked as manual override.
  • For knockout matches, the portal can auto-fill winners/losers into linked future matches.
